About me

I have strong HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript skills that I still try to develop and experience implementing Responsive Web Design (i.e. desktop, tablet, smartphone friendly). I keep track of W3C standards and HTML5 Living Standard. I know the basics of SEO and Accessibility. I always write a clean and readable code. Most time I am programming in JavaScript. I increase my knowledge and improve my skills every single day. I have a dream and it is to become well-known and respected person in the world of Information Technology.
